Breadboard 🚀

📘 Introduction

Breadboard ek electronic board hai jisme soldering ke bina circuit banaya jata hai.

📍 Pins Information

Breadboard me rows aur columns connected hote hain for easy wiring.

⚡ Voltage & Current

Normally 3.3V aur 5V circuits ke liye use hota hai.

🖼️ Component Diagram

Breadboard wiring aur connection diagram yahan show hoga.

🌍 Applications

Arduino projects, testing circuits aur prototyping me use hota hai.

🚀 Advantages

Easy wiring, reusable aur beginner friendly.
