Basic Components 🚀

🔌 Arduino UNO

Arduino Uno ek powerful microcontroller board hai jo beginners aur advanced projects dono ke liye use hota hai.

📟 Breadboard

Breadboard ek solderless board hai jisme circuits temporary test aur build kiye jate hain.

💡 LED

LED ek light emitting component hai jo Arduino projects me indication aur lighting ke liye use hota hai.

🎚️ Resistor

Resistor current ko control karta hai aur electronic circuits ko safe banata hai.

⚡ Capacitor

Capacitor electric charge ko store karta hai aur filtering aur timing circuits me use hota hai.

🔘 Push Button

Push Button ek input device hai jo press karne par signal bhejta hai.

🎛️ Potentiometer

Potentiometer ek variable resistor hai jo voltage aur values adjust karne ke liye use hota hai.

➡️ Diode

Diode current ko sirf ek direction me flow karne deta hai.

🔋 Transistor

Transistor switching aur amplification ke liye electronic circuits me use hota hai.

🧵 Jumper Wires

Jumper wires components ko connect karne ke liye Arduino circuits me use hote hain.

🔋 Battery

Battery Arduino aur electronic circuits ko portable power supply deti hai.

🔊 Buzzer

Buzzer sound produce karta hai aur alerts aur notifications ke liye use hota hai.
