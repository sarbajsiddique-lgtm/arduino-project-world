Transistor 🚀

📘 Introduction

Transistor ek semiconductor device hai jo switching aur amplification ke liye use hota hai.

📍 Pins Information

Transistor ke 3 pins hote hain — Base, Collector aur Emitter.

⚡ Voltage & Current

Low voltage signal se high current devices control kiye jaate hain.

🖼️ Component Diagram

NPN aur PNP transistor connection diagram yahan show hoga.

🌍 Applications

Motor control, switching circuits, amplifiers aur robotics me use hota hai.

🚀 Advantages

Fast switching, compact size aur low power consumption.
